Mission Statement

The AIC Fredericton is dedicated to fostering a vibrant, inclusive, and engaged community. Our mission is to serve the community by promoting cultural heritage, supporting community members, and creating opportunities for education, understanding, and connection. Guided by values of integrity, inclusivity, and accountability, we strive to preserve cultural traditions while supporting the growth and well-being of present and future generations.

Note: The association will not seek to obtain personal gains for any of its members; any money or property that might accrue to the association will be solely utilized in the furtherance of its objectives.

Our Core Pillars

  • **Cultural Preservation:** Implementing programs that preserve and promote cultural heritage, such as language instruction, traditional arts workshops, and cultural celebrations.
  • **Community Support:** Providing mentorship, networking opportunities, and social programming to support community members in building strong connections and personal development.
  • **Education and Outreach:** Engaging in public education initiatives to promote cross-cultural understanding and awareness within the broader Fredericton community.
