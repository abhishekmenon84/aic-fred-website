Who We Are

Building Bridges,
Celebrating Heritage

AIC Fredericton is dedicated to fostering a vibrant, inclusive, and engaged community. Our mission is to serve the community by promoting cultural heritage, supporting community members, and creating opportunities for education, understanding, and connection.

Guided by values of integrity, inclusivity, and accountability, we strive to preserve cultural traditions while supporting the growth and well-being of present and future generations of Indo-Canadians in New Brunswick.

Our Roots

Cultural Heritage

🎵

Music & Dance

From classical Bharatanatyam and Kathak to folk dances like Bhangra and Garba — we keep our performing arts alive through workshops and performances.

🍛

Cuisine & Food Culture

Food is at the heart of Indian culture. Our potlucks, cooking demos, and food festivals bring the flavours of India to Fredericton's table.

🪔

Festivals & Celebrations

Diwali, Holi, Eid, Navratri, Onam, and more — we celebrate India's incredible diversity of festivals with the whole Fredericton community.

🧵

Language & Literature

Language connects us to our roots. We support Hindi, Tamil, Punjabi, and other language initiatives to help future generations stay connected.

🎨

Arts & Crafts

Rangoli, Mehndi, pottery, and traditional textile arts — we celebrate India's rich artistic heritage through exhibitions and workshops.

📖

Oral History & Stories

Stories passed down through generations are our living archive. We document, share, and celebrate the journeys of Indo-Canadian families in New Brunswick.
